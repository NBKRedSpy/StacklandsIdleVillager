[h1]Stacklands Idle Villager[/h1]

Adds a red border around idle food producers or villagers.


[h1]Settings[/h1]

The mod options includes settings to enable or disable the highlighting of the following idle cards:
[list]
[*]Villagers
[*]Farms and Gardens
[*]Fishing Spots
[*]Fishing Traps
[*]Greenhouses
[*]Poop
[/list]
